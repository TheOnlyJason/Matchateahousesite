# CONSTANCE Matcha Teahouse

## 🍵 About

CONSTANCE is a premium matcha teahouse located in San Francisco's Richmond District. This digital flagship site tells our story through six immersive chapters, from the tea fields of Uji, Japan to our studio at 3512 Balboa Street.

## ✨ Experience Highlights

### Story First
Unlike traditional e-commerce sites, we prioritize storytelling. Each scroll reveals a new chapter in matcha's journey from leaf to cup.

### Cinematic Design
Inspired by award-winning platforms like Adaline.ai, featuring:
- Scroll-triggered video scrubbing
- Real-time particle simulations
- Glassmorphic UI elements
- Smooth momentum scrolling

### Stone-Themed Aesthetic
Warm whites and stone greys evoke the tranquility of Japanese tea ceremonies, creating a digital sanctuary that honors tradition while embracing modernity.
